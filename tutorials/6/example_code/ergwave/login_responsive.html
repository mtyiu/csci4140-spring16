REGISTERED USER

Logging in as a registered user indicates you have read and accepted the Acceptable Use Policy.

  • Enter your UNIX username and password and select your dept from the FQDN drop-down box.
  • We are recommend to enable the WEP to encrypt your WLAN session, switch to SSID ERGWAVE3 and enable the WEP. For the WEP key,check the webpage http://www.erg.cuhk.edu.hk/wlan/ or click the link after login.
  • For more info and other encryption method available, you can go to http://info.wlan.erg.cuhk.edu.hk in the wireless network. To acceee the info page in wired network, use the URL http://www.erg.cuhk.edu.hk/wlan/ instead.
